FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need a visa to visit Sri Lanka? +
Most nationalities require an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) which can be obtained online before arrival. It typically costs USD 35 for tourists and allows a 30-day stay. We're happy to guide you through the process.
When is the best time to visit Sri Lanka? +
Sri Lanka is a year-round destination. The west and south coast (Galle, Mirissa) are best from November to April. The east coast (Trincomalee, Arugam Bay) shines from May to September. The hill country is pleasant year-round. We'll tailor your itinerary to the optimal time.
What's included in your tour packages? +
Most packages include accommodation, all transfers in an air-conditioned vehicle, an English-speaking guide, entrance fees, and breakfast. Flights are not included but we can recommend and assist with booking. Full details are provided for each specific package.
Can you customize the itinerary to our preferences? +
Absolutely — customization is at the heart of what we do. Our itineraries are starting points. We adjust pace, accommodation style, activities, and destinations completely to your wishes. Use our trip planner above or contact us directly.
Is Sri Lanka safe for tourists? +
Yes, Sri Lanka is generally very safe for tourists and is known for its warm hospitality. The local people are incredibly welcoming. Like any travel destination, standard precautions apply, and our team is always available to advise on safe areas and practices.
What is your cancellation policy? +
We offer flexible cancellation options. Cancellations made 60+ days before departure receive a full refund less a small admin fee. 30–60 days: 50% refund. Under 30 days: non-refundable but fully transferable to a future date within 12 months. Full details are in our booking terms.

Our Beginning

Born from a passion for this extraordinary island

Serendip Go was founded in 2003 by Rohan Silva, a Sri Lankan travel enthusiast who believed that the world deserved to experience his home island beyond the tourist brochures. Starting with just three dedicated team members and a single 4x4, we've grown into one of Sri Lanka's most trusted inbound travel companies.

Today, our team of 40 passionate travel professionals — all deeply rooted in Sri Lankan culture and geography — craft journeys for thousands of international visitors each year. From honeymooners in boutique Galle hotels to wildlife photographers in Yala, we serve every kind of traveller.

We are proud members of the Sri Lanka Tourism Alliance, certified by the Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority, and winners of the Island's Choice Travel Award for seven consecutive years.
