For over two decades, Serendip Go has been introducing the world to the wonder, warmth, and beauty of Sri Lanka — one tailor-made adventure at a time.

Our Beginning

Born from a passion for this extraordinary island

Serendip Go was founded in 2003 by Rohan Silva, a Sri Lankan travel enthusiast who believed that the world deserved to experience his home island beyond the tourist brochures. Starting with just three dedicated team members and a single 4x4, we've grown into one of Sri Lanka's most trusted inbound travel companies.

Today, our team of 40 passionate travel professionals — all deeply rooted in Sri Lankan culture and geography — craft journeys for thousands of international visitors each year. From honeymooners in boutique Galle hotels to wildlife photographers in Yala, we serve every kind of traveller.

We are proud members of the Sri Lanka Tourism Alliance, certified by the Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority, and winners of the Island's Choice Travel Award for seven consecutive years.
